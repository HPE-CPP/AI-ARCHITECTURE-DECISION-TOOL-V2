# AI Chatbot Requirements

## Overview

We need an internal AI chatbot for our team of 50 employees. The chatbot will answer questions from our internal documentation and policy guides. The dataset is small, around 500 documents total. We update the documentation monthly.

## Requirements

Responses must be under 1 second. Accuracy is important because employees rely on this for compliance procedures. The system should be deployed on our private cloud. Cost efficiency matters since we have a small IT budget.

## Users

Around 50 internal users. Queries are low volume, maybe 100 per day total. The domain is specialized to our internal company procedures.
